Chatpati Broccoli Recipe

Ingredients:

250 gm broccoli-cut small
2 Tbsp oil
2 tsp mustard seeds
4-5 curry leaves
3-4 whole red pepper
1/8 tsp asafoetida powder
1 tsp cumin
50 gm ginger-shredded fine
2 tsp garlic-chopped fine
20 gm tamarind-soaked in 1 cup water and strained
2 tsp salt or to taste

Method:

Heat oil and add mustard, curry leaves, red pepper, asafoetida and cumin.  
When seeds start to splutter, add the ginger and garlic and saute till a light brown.  
Add the broccoli and saute over high heat a few times, then lower the heat, cover and cook till bite like.  
Add tamarind and salt, let it come to a boil and serve hot.
